The ChatGPT Team

The ChatGPT Team is a group of interns working on the development of artificial intelligence using the GPT model. This team consists of interns from the MSIB 5 program (Internship and Independent Study Batch 5, 2023) in partnership with Ditjen Diktiristek.

  • The team developed an adaptive, interactive, and personalized English learning application called PEL (Personalized English Learning)
  • The application is free and can be used by everyone
  • The application utilizes the OpenAI Chat Completion Model API, specifically gpt-3.5-turbo
